About.

Featuring garden views, Apartment Asgard provides accommodation with a balcony, located around 12 km from Plitvice Lakes National Park - Entrance 2.

The 4-star apartment features mountain views and is 9.2 km from Plitvice Lakes National Park - Entrance 1.

Including air-conditioning, the apartment consists of 1 bedroom, a living room, a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and a coffee machine, and 1 bathroom with a bidet and free toiletries.

Providing free private parking, this 4-star apartment also features free WiFi throughout the property.

The apartment offers 4-star accommodation with a hot tub and terrace.

Jezerce - Mukinje Bus Station is 14 km from Apartment Asgard. The nearest airport is Zagreb Franjo Tuđman Airport, 132 km from the accommodation.

This accommodation is best suited for couples.
